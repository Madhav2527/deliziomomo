Our Story

A Journey From
Cart to Cult

What started as a humble food cart on the vibrant streets of Dombivli in 2011 has blossomed into one of Mumbai's most cherished momo destinations. Delizio Momo was born from a passion for authentic flavors and the belief that every bite should tell a story.

Over 13 years, we've perfected the art of momo-making — from the delicate folds of our steamed momos to the satisfying crunch of our signature Kurkure variety. Every dumpling is handcrafted with love, using recipes that blend traditional Tibetan techniques with Mumbai's vibrant street food culture.
